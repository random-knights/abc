# Lab 10: Earth Data Boundaries

## Goal

Design a compact evidence summary from Earth-oriented Python samples without
downloading or committing raw imagery.

## Concepts

- provider/source metadata
- raw imagery boundary
- compact artifact
- attribution
- limitations

## Setup

Use these files for review only:

- `assets/python/earth-vision.py`
- `assets/python/nasa-vision.py`

Create:

```text
workspace/earth-data-boundaries/evidence-summary.md
```

## Steps

1. Identify provider/source names.
2. Find credential placeholders.
3. Find any imagery or file-writing behavior.
4. Draft a compact summary with source, region, target, confidence, and
   limitations.
5. Add a note: raw imagery is not committed.

## Challenge

Write a one-paragraph explanation of why evidence summaries are safer than raw
imagery for beginner workflows.

## Expected Outcome

You can connect Earth data examples to source-backed reporting without exposing
secrets, raw imagery, or sensitive coordinates.
