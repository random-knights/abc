# Candidate Profile Template

Fill in the PROFILE constant in job-scorer.js with your own background,
or export it as the CANDIDATE_PROFILE environment variable.

## Background
Years of experience, focus areas, current career direction.

## Salary
- Remote minimum: $X
- Hybrid minimum: $Y (N days max on-site)
- Dealbreakers: [e.g. 5-day on-site, below $X]

## Tech Stack
Primary, secondary, AI/LLM tooling, CI/CD, cloud platforms.

## Goals
Target roles and the reason for the move.

## Strengths
What you bring that most candidates do not.

## Gaps
Be honest -- the scorer flags these against job requirements.

## Mission Preferences
Industries and company missions that energize you vs ones that do not.
